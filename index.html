01

The Story of UNITED VII

UNITED VII is not just a clothing line — it's a movement born from friendship, creativity, and the dream of building something bigger than ourselves.

Our name carries meaning: seven silhouettes standing arm-in-arm, representing unity, individuality, and strength. The Roman numeral twist, VII, gives our brand a timeless identity, tying our journey to heritage and resilience.

We didn't start with factories or big budgets. We started with ideas, late-night talks, and inspiration from the brands we admired. DOSMILL2000, ORIGIN, HOOLIGANS — each collection is our own twist on those influences, transformed into apparel that speaks for us.

Behind the brand are seven names, seven voices, seven dreams:

  • Jerick Ventosollada
  • Jhay Verdadero
  • Rayver Susas
  • Mark Lawrence Navidad
  • Nashlie Sumayao
  • John Lawrence A. Tuyor
  • Estivan James Dejesus

Together, we are UNITED VII.

02

The Collections

DOSMILL2000 — DOSMIL JULY POP-UP 2024 The Dos Mil pop-up event on July 20, 2024, at Kalawakan Spacetime in Quezon City was a huge success ... Thank you to everyone who pulled up and helped make this pop-up such a meaningful and unforgettable experience..

ORIGIN — Origin Manila clothing stands as a vibrant embodiment of Filipino hip-hop culture –– known for its distinct flair and bold expression. Frequently worn by the trio and their producer BRGR, the brand has become synonymous with streetwear that speaks volumes, as each piece is marked with the signature ORIGIN, proudly displayed across acid-washed jeans, camouflaged sweatshirts, and specialized ombre jackets..

HOOLIGANS — Hooligans, established in 2016, is a brand that really speaks to the youth of the Philippines through its fashion-forward designs. Initially, the brand made a name for itself by tapping into the skatewear trend, which was a huge hit among the skater crowd. As it grew in popularity, it wasn't long before the hip hop scene took notice and started adopting Hooligans' style as well. This shift allowed the brand to gain a broader audience, securing its place in the diverse world of youth fashion.

03

The Vision

Our vision is simple but powerful: to create apparel that represents unity while celebrating individuality. Clothing that doesn't just look good — it tells a story.

UNITED VII is more than fashion. It's a statement, a lifestyle, and a community. And soon, the world will see it.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

We want you to love what you ordered. If something isn't right, here's how our return and exchange process works:

  • Contact us within 14 days of receiving your order via Instagram DM @rencee.oi or email support@unitedvii.ph.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.
  • We'll provide a return shipping label or arrange a pickup for Metro Manila orders.
  • Once received and inspected, your exchange or refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Sale items, limited edition, and LTD pieces are final sale and are not eligible for returns.
